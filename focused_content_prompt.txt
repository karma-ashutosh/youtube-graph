## FOCUS: Quality over Quantity

**Only extract concepts that are ACTUALLY EXPLAINED in this segment.**

❌ **DO NOT include:**
- Terms mentioned in passing without explanation
- Prerequisites the speaker assumes you know
- Buzzwords or jargon used casually (e.g., "B2B", "SaaS", "RevOps")
- Brief references to other topics
- Names of tools/companies unless they're used as teaching examples

✅ **DO include:**
- Concepts that get definitions, explanations, or examples
- Ideas that are developed with some depth
- Supporting concepts that are necessary to understand the main topic
- Teaching points that add value

## EXTRACTION GUIDELINES

### PRIMARY CONCEPT (Required)
**What is the MAIN topic being explained or taught?**
- There is ONE primary concept per segment
- It should be explained with depth (`comprehensive` or `partial`)
- This is what someone would learn from watching this segment

**Coverage Depth (2 options only):**
- `comprehensive` = Thoroughly explained with definitions, examples, nuance
- `partial` = Explained with some depth but not exhaustive

**Explanation Type:**
- `definition` = Defines what the concept is
- `case_study` = Real-world example or company story
- `how_to` = Step-by-step practical guidance
- `comparison` = Contrasts with other concepts
- `metaphor` = Uses analogy/metaphor to explain
- `story` = Narrative or anecdote

---

### SUPPORTING CONCEPTS (Optional, 2-5 max)
**What concepts are EXPLAINED to help understand the primary concept?**

⚠️ **Strict criteria:**
- Only include if the speaker actually EXPLAINS it (not just mentions)
- Must have `comprehensive` or `partial` coverage
- Should contribute meaningfully to understanding the primary concept

For each supporting concept:
1. **Name** - Clear, specific concept name
2. **Role** - How it helps explain the primary concept (1-2 sentences)
3. **Coverage** - Must be `comprehensive` or `partial`
